Welcome to Synthex! To create a Synthex account, follow these steps:

  1. Review the requirements.
  2. Review the agreements.
  3. Contact us requesting an account.

Requirements:

  • You must have a valid Discord account.
  • You must not have been recently blacklisted from Synthex services.

Agreements:

If you fail to uphold these agreements you will be permanently blacklisted from our services.

  • You agree not to abuse or unlawfully use our services
  • You agree not to leak or resell Synthex applications and assets.
  • You agree that our administrators have the permission to terminate your account based on suspicion or reports.

Contact Information:
