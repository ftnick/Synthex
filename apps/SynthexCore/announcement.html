Important Announcement Regarding SynthexCore Version 2.0.0 Revamp

Introduction

Dear Members,

Due to community input and feedback, the SynthexCore application will be revamped into version 2.0.0.

What's New

  • New UI Styles (New navigation, Tabs, Setting.)
  • Better downtime communication (Including more notification options. You will also not be auto logged out if the program is outdated.)
  • New installer design (No more weird console apps, the installer will be completely changed to include more flexible options and customization.)
  • Full bug wipe and revamp for services (All services will either be revamped in accordance with the 'New UI Styles' or changed/removed.)
  • Discontinuation of active development for Version 0.0.18. Bug fixes and further development will cease, but the version will remain usable after the revamp.

More to come as we continue to develop new features along the way.

How to Reach Us

Want to file a bug report, need some help, or have a suggestion? Reach out to us on Discord at ftnick or visit our support page.
