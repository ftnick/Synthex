Upcoming Game: Echos

In the realm of Echos, our protagonist Lucy, a teenage girl, openly admits to being a deceiver. She weaves a web of lies, concealing the truth from everyone around her. Lucy assures us that she's on the verge of revealing the real story behind the demise of her companion Max, who meets a gruesome end in their senior year. Yet, the lingering question remains: Can Lucy be trusted to disclose the actual truth?
